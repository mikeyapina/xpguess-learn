What Is XPGuess?

XPGuess is an educational sports learning platform that helps people understand how real football (soccer) decisions and transfer markets work.

XPGuess uses simple Yes or No questions to explain official outcomes based on verified announcements, not rumors.

How XPGuess Works

Each question is resolved using official club, league, or governing body sources. No real money is involved.

Topics Covered

  • Football transfers
  • FIFA governance and regulations
  • January 2026 transfer window
  • Sports decision-making

Why XPGuess Exists

XPGuess exists to help people learn how professional sports systems function in a transparent, educational way.
