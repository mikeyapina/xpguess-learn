What Is XPGuess?

XPGuess is an educational sports learning platform that helps people understand how football decisions and transfer markets work.

XPGuess uses simple Yes or No questions to explain official outcomes based on verified announcements.

How XPGuess Works

Each question is resolved using official club, league, or governing body sources.
