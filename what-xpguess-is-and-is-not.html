What XPGuess Is — and Is Not

XPGuess is an educational platform designed to help people learn and measure decision-making through structured, hypothetical scenarios.

This page exists to clearly define the scope, safeguards, and intended use of XPGuess for users, families, investors, platforms, and reviewers.

Plain-Language Purpose

XPGuess helps users understand how decisions work in complex systems—such as professional sports, governance, and timing-based outcomes—without involving real money or financial risk.

All scenarios on XPGuess are hypothetical and designed for learning purposes only.

What XPGuess Does

XPGuess provides a structured environment where users:

  • Answer clear Yes / No questions based on real-world decision frameworks
  • Review outcomes using verified, public information
  • Track learning progress using XP (Experience Points)
  • Analyze patterns and decision quality over time

AI may be used to analyze participation patterns and generate educational insights, but it does not act on a user’s behalf or execute decisions.

What XPGuess Explicitly Does Not Do

XPGuess does not:

  • Accept deposits or hold user funds
  • Execute trades, wagers, or transactions
  • Promise or imply financial returns
  • Generate income for users
  • Operate as a trading bot or automated investment system

XP (Experience Points) are not money, not a currency, and not redeemable for cash.

Educational & Compliance Positioning

XPGuess operates in the same category as educational simulators, analytics tools, and decision-training platforms.

It is not:

  • A broker
  • An exchange
  • A custodian
  • A financial advisor
  • A financial product

The platform is intentionally designed to remain non-custodial, non-financial, and education-first.

Adult Use & Future Versions

Future versions of XPGuess for adult users may introduce more advanced analytics, deeper scenario complexity, and expanded educational tooling.

These versions will maintain the same foundational safeguards: no custody, no execution, no financial incentives, and no automated transactions.

Why These Boundaries Matter

Clear boundaries protect users, families, investors, and the platform itself.

By defining scope intentionally, XPGuess can scale responsibly across education, sports, and decision science—without exposing users to financial risk or regulatory confusion.
