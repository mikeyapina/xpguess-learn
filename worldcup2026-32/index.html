📐 The Framework

Step 1: Measure the Gate (SPI)

Did expected teams advance?
First we check if Matchday 3 preserved the competitive ordering. Result: 83.3% — the gate held in 20 of 24 fixtures.

Step 2: Classify the Gate (GEI)

One metric, three thresholds.
GEI = Top Team's Goal Share %.

Dominant >60% — Winner overpowered
Suppressed 45-60% — Winner in flat group
Open <45% + SPI held
Inverted <45% + SPI broke

Step 3: Test Carryover (TCI)

Does group environment predict knockout success?
Testing whether teams from certain GEI types perform differently in knockouts.

Status: In progress.
