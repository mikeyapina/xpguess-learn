Earn XP on XPGuess

This page explains how XP (Experience Points) are earned inside XPGuess. XP is used to track educational participation and learning progress.

XPGuess uses XP to help people understand how real football (soccer) decisions work. The platform is educational and does not involve real money.

How XP Is Earned

XP is earned by participating in educational activities on the platform. The primary method is answering verified Yes or No questions.

Questions may reference both the January (winter) transfer window and the summer transfer window, depending on when official decisions occur.

Question Format

Each XPGuess question presents a clear statement with two possible answers: Yes or No.

Questions are written in advance and include a defined resolution date and criteria.

How XP Is Verified

XPGuess questions are resolved using official public sources such as:

  • Club announcements
  • League registrations
  • Governing body confirmations
  • Official regulatory decisions

XPGuess does not use rumors, leaks, or unofficial reporting to resolve questions.

The Busking System

XPGuess also supports a system called Busking. Busking allows verified social or performance activity to be converted into XP.

This may include activity such as verified engagement or followers on external platforms. XP is awarded as educational progress only.

Busking XP is not money and does not represent payments or payouts.

Educational Purpose

XPGuess exists to support learning and understanding of football systems, including transfer windows, governance, and professional decision-making.

The platform is not gambling and does not involve real money.
