How XPGuess Works

XPGuess is an educational platform designed to help people understand how real football decisions and events unfold over time.

The platform uses simple Yes or No questions to focus on clarity, timing, and official outcomes rather than speculation or rumors.

Question Format

Each XPGuess question presents a clear statement with two possible answers: Yes or No.

Questions are written in advance and include a defined resolution date and criteria.

How Outcomes Are Resolved

Outcomes are resolved using official public sources such as:

  • Club announcements
  • League registrations
  • Governing body confirmations
  • Official regulatory decisions

XPGuess does not use rumors, leaks, or unofficial reporting to resolve questions.

Educational Purpose

XPGuess exists to support learning and understanding of football systems, including transfer windows, governance, and decision-making processes.

The platform is not gambling and does not involve real money.
