Earn XP on XPGuess

This page explains how XP (Experience Points) are earned inside XPGuess. XP is an educational progress metric used to track learning, participation, and verified activity across the platform.

XPGuess is educational, not gambling. The platform does not involve real money, wagering, prizes, equity, investment credits, or financial outcomes. XP has no cash value.

What XP Means

XP (Experience Points) represent progress in understanding how real football (soccer) decisions work — including official outcomes, transfer markets, governance, and timing.

  • XP is not money and is not redeemable for cash.
  • XP is used to measure learning progress and system participation.
  • XP supports transparent progress tracking for learners, families, and educators.

Ways to Earn XP

1) Answer Verified Yes / No Questions

The primary way to earn XP is by participating in XPGuess questions. Each question is a clear statement with two possible answers: Yes or No.

Questions are written in advance and include a defined open window, close window, and resolution criteria. XPGuess questions focus on clarity and official outcomes — not speculation or rumors.

2) Learn From Official Outcomes

XPGuess questions are resolved using official public sources such as:

  • Club announcements
  • League registrations
  • Governing body confirmations
  • Official regulatory decisions

XPGuess does not use rumors, leaks, or unofficial reporting to resolve questions.

3) Complete Learning Modules (When Available)

Some XP can be earned through structured educational content, such as explainers and walkthroughs that teach how professional football systems function. Modules may include transfer-market education, governance basics, and decision-making training.

The Busking System (XP Conversion From Verified Social Activity)

XPGuess also supports an optional pathway called Busking. Busking is designed for participants who build real momentum through public performance and social platforms.

In Busking mode, XPGuess can convert verified social activity signals (for example: verified engagement activity, followers, or verified reach) into XP for system progress.

  • This conversion is a non-cash educational credit used to recognize consistency and verified activity.
  • It is designed to reflect how platforms recognize engagement inside their own ecosystems, without introducing cash rewards.
  • XPGuess does not provide money, payouts, investment credits, equity, or financial rewards through Busking XP.

To read more about Busking in the XPGuess ecosystem, visit: Busking System

Educational Purpose

XPGuess exists to support learning and understanding of professional sports systems, including transfer windows, governance, and decision-making processes. XP is used to measure educational progress and participation.

The platform is not gambling and does not involve real money.
