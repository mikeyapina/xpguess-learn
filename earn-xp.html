How XP Is Earned on XPGuess

XP on XPGuess represents participation, consistency, and learning within structured sports and development systems. It is not a financial reward, performance score, or prediction of future success.

XP exists to document engagement with real-world rules, timelines, and decision-making processes that govern professional and developmental sports environments.

What XP Represents

XP functions as a standardized participation signal. It reflects how consistently a participant engages with educational content, governed processes, and structured learning activities.

XP does not represent athletic ability, medical outcomes, or competitive ranking.

How XP Is Earned

  • Participating in educational questions and learning prompts
  • Engaging with verified sports governance scenarios
  • Completing structured learning flows over time
  • Maintaining consistency across learning sessions
  • Participating in approved training or support modules (non-medical)

What XP Is Not

  • XP is not money or a financial instrument
  • XP is not gambling or wagering
  • XP does not predict career outcomes
  • XP is not a medical or psychological metric

This distinction allows XPGuess to operate safely across age groups, sports, and jurisdictions, while remaining aligned with educational and governance standards.
